Goal and Objectives

Our main goal is to help out people in providing healthcare information and resources,

our other motive is also to educate and spread awareness about the health care facilities in our country.

Our aim is also to collaborate with various hospitals to cater to the public’s health care necesseties.

This is a non-profit trust whose main aim is only to educate people and help them, providing legitimate resources and information about the medical facilities in Bangalore.
